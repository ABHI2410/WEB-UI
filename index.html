About FAt 

FAT is a Free online platform for all the farmers to sell the crops Directly to the customers without any middle man in between to  Maximise farmers profit. 

FAT is based on Blockchain technology for Secure network based communication and transation between farmers and customers.

FAT allows farmers from any region of India to sell their Crops, Fruits and Flowers to anyone who need them accross the Country.
